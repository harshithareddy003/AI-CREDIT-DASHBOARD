Basel III is a global regulatory framework designed to strengthen regulation, supervision, and risk management in the banking sector.

Key Components:
- Capital Adequacy: Ensures banks hold sufficient capital to cover their risks.
- Liquidity Coverage Ratio (LCR): Requires banks to hold enough liquid assets to survive a 30-day stress scenario.
- Net Stable Funding Ratio (NSFR): Promotes stable funding structures over a longer-term horizon.
